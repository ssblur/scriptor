# Nominomist Villagers

On occasion, it seems that you can find Nominomists in villages.

These Villagers will trade a number of resources that are useful in writing spell tomes,
as well as trading tomes themselves!
Specifically, they are more than willing to trade a couple of lower-tier tomes for one of my higher-tier ones.

Could be useful if I have a few higher-tier tomes around, like from defeating powerful monsters...
