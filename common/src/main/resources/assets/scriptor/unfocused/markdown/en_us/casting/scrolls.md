# Scrolls

Sometimes, I know I'll want to cast a spell, but it's not worth making a whole Spellbook over.

<item href="scriptor:unwritten_scroll"></item>
When the time calls for it, a Scroll is handy to have.
These can be written at a [Writing Desk](scriptor:tools/writing_desk), and let one cast a spell
with the convenience of a tome.

Better yet, they seem to be able to cast even more powerful spells than tomes!
Shame they are consumed after just one use, though...
