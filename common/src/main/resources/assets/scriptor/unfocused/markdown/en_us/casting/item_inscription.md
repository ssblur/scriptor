# Inscribing Items

It's so simple! 
I can't believe I never thought to even check before!

Just like enchanted books can be imbued into a piece of equipment at an Anvil, a spellbook's spell
can be inscribed into an item by placing them together on one.

Normally, this just results in an item which can cast the inscribed spell.
Fantastic on its own, but I wonder if this could be used to replicate the effects of enchanted items or armor?

I have seen the occasional Pillager with a stronger-than-usual crossbow, maybe one of those holds more clues?
