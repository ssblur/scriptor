# Casting Lecterns

Lecterns are a way to automatically cast a spell from a 
[Spellbook](scriptor:casting/tome_casting).

They can only cast spells roughly 1/3rd as powerful as the limit of spells cast by a player, give or take,
and will automatically cast the spell of the book placed on them as soon as they are able.
To place a Spellbook into them, just use the lectern with the book in your hand.
To take it out, just use the lectern with nothing in your hand.

By default, their inventory includes any chests placed next to them, and they will cast spells behind them,
towards the back of the spellbook.

Their **focus**, which includes their inventory for casting and the target of any self-targeting spells, 
can be modified using [Casting Crystals](scriptor:casting/lecterns/casting_crystals).
