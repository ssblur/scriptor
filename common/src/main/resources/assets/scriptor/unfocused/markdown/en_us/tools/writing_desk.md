# Writing Desk

After writing a few tomes, I tired of trying to remember and write words one by one.
I already have a Scriptionary, why not use it?

<item href="scriptor:writing_table"></item>
The Writing Desk is a handy tool which lets you read and write Spellbooks with (relative) ease!

While a Scriptionary is in the top slot, placing a written Book or Spellbook in the other slot gives you
the option to right click on any word in the book to add an entry to your Scriptionary!
Just add a description so you remember what the word does, and it'll be available in your Scriptionary from
now on.

But that's not all!

If you put a Book and Quill or Spellbook and Quill into the bottom slot instead, you can use write directly from
the Scriptionary.
Simply click on the word from the list to the right, and it'll be inserted (with spaces if appropriate)
into the book you're writing, at the cursor.

Then, when you're ready to go, click the Sign button and you can finalize your book, easy-peasy!

Oh! And before I forget!

If you have any Scraps lying around that you haven't already stuffed into your Scriptionary, you can 
put one of them in the bottom slot to add an entry to your Scriptionary without needing to add it in manually.
