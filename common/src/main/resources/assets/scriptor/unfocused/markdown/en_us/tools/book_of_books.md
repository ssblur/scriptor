# Book of Books

In my travels, I've picked up a number of different spells.
While these are quite useful, they also take up a lot of space in my pockets.

To this end, I've created the Book of Books!
<item href="scriptor:book_of_books"></item>
This wondrous little tool can compress a number of different books down into just one.

Simple hold a Spellbook in your cursor, then right click on the Book of Books with it to insert it,
and right click with an empty cursor to remove a book.
Then you can scroll while sneaking to cycle between contained books, 
and use the Book of Books to cast them. 

Simple!
