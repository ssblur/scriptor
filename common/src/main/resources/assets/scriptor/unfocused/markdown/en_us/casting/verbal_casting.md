# Verbal Casting

If you're like me, just getting into the magic of Nominomy, then welcome!
Verbal casting is probably the easiest place to start if you're getting your proverbial legs under you.

Some of the first spells I found were in [tomes](scriptor:casting/tome_casting), but the first spells I were
able to cast were using my very own voice.

By simply speaking aloud the very words I'd read in those tomes, I was able to cast spells to the same effects.
This even allowed me to make modifications to spells before I wrote them down in tomes once I'd started to craft
my own.

This sort of casting is not without consequence, however. 
Once a spell is cast like this, I'd often find myself consumed by a hunger, or worse yet, in profound pain. 
Luckily it never lasted long, but I dread what could happen if I were to cast a stronger spell thoughtlessly.

I've also heard tell of a potion that can be consumed to cast spells more readily, though I've never seen 
it before. 
Maybe if I were more versed in the Alchimiaecal arts, but alas.
