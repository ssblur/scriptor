# Spellbook Obfuscation

I've discovered a way by which secrets can be kept from other Nominomists, 
maybe for dangerous spells or the like.

By combining a [Spellbook](scriptor:casting/tome_casting) with a Glow Ink Sac in a crafting table,
you can effectively obfuscate it, preventing it from being read.
The words that make up the spell will move around when in a mage's hand, and won't show up at all when
placed in a lectern or a [Writing Desk](scriptor:tools/writing_desk).

Thankfully, it doesn't seem that any of the mages of old whose works I've found utilized this technique,
otherwise I might have had a bit more trouble discovering spells.
